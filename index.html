Visible Judicial Condition in China

The government of China has published justice data to people in order to let people know more about the government and laws. However, most of people have no concept for these data because of lack of professional knowledge.

For ordinary people, they can easily understand data from map and select data which they are intersted in such as find data from a province where they live in.

For lawyers and professional staffs, the visialization is classified by case cause, year and court which could facilitate them to learn more about judicial condition in China.
